Security

Security is of the uttermost importance to us, as it is to you.

We store all your files in secure indices, ensuring a strict no-access rule for all your data.
All traffic between your servers and ours is encrypted using SSL.

A chaos-monkey script runs continuously trying to breach our system, ensuring your data-policies are correctly applied.

Finally, your original document is never stored on a hard drive: we only keep it in RAM for the duration of our analysis, then discard the file and only keep our secured index data: you remain the sole owner of the original document.
